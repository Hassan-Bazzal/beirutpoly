About Beirut Poly

Beirut Poly is a forward-thinking institution dedicated to shaping the future of engineering, science, and technology education in Lebanon and the Middle East. Located in the vibrant city of Beirut, it provides students with hands-on learning, cutting-edge research opportunities, and strong ties to the tech industry.
